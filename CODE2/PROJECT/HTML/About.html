What is Hackathon 2024?

Hackathon 2024 is an exciting event hosted by Deepak and his team, bringing together passionate developers, designers, and creative thinkers. It's an opportunity to work collaboratively and solve real-world problems through innovative coding solutions.

Whether you're a seasoned developer or just getting started, Hackathon 2024 has something for everyone. Join us for a weekend filled with learning, building, and networking with the tech community.
